El Castillo in Port D'Addaia on the wonderful island of Menorca is a very popular
Bar/Bistro/Restaurant.
Open from May until September, we have been welcoming visitors for over 10 years.
Our new app, allows you to peruse our menu, book a table, email or phone us.
It also gives you directions to find us, read about our facilities, and even see who is serving you.
Use the app to book a table for our popular 'Fish And Chip' night, or any other time.
Connect to our Facebook page, and TripAdvisor pages.
The app supports both English and Spanish - simply click on the flag to switch between English and Spanish.
